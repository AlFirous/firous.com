My parents decide transform small parts of the garden as chicken coop. To avoid the smell and future proof, half of the coop is permanently build with concrete instead of using all woods. The roof is transparent plastic for sunlight come through, of course there is place to hide. It is separated into 2 rooms with each door, one for the _meat_ and the other one is for _eggs_.
